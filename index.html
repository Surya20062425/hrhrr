Wedding Rituals

Sacred ceremonies of Telugu tradition

🎉

Pellikuthuru

The bride is adorned with turmeric and oil, surrounded by family songs and blessings.

🌺

Kashi Yatra

The groom pretends to leave for Kashi until the bride's father convinces him to stay and marry.

🥥

Jeelakarra Bellam

The couple shares cumin and jaggery paste, symbolizing the bittersweet journey of marriage.

👣

Saptapadi

The seven sacred steps around the holy fire, each step a vow for their new life together.

💍

Mangala Sutra

The sacred yellow thread is tied around the bride's neck, marking the eternal bond of marriage.

🎊

Grihapravesham

The bride enters her new home, kicking a pot of rice to symbolize prosperity and abundance.
